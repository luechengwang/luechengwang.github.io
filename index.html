Research Interests

My research centers on figurative meaning in language, particularly metaphor, and its computational modeling in large language and multimodal systems.

Areas of focus:

  • Internal mechanisms of metaphor processing in large language models
  • Evaluation and interpretation of metaphor understanding
  • Multimodal processing of figurative expressions
